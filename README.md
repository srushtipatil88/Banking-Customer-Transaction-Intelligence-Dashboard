## Credit Card Financial Dashboard –

This project is an interactive **Credit Card Financial Dashboard** developed to analyze customer behavior, transaction trends, revenue generation, and financial performance using data visualization techniques. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of credit card operations and helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

The project consists of two main dashboards:

---

# 1. Credit Card Transaction Report Dashboard

The **Transaction Report Dashboard** focuses on analyzing transaction activity, revenue generation, card usage patterns, and customer spending behavior.

### Key Features:

* Displays overall KPIs such as:

  * **Total Revenue:** 55.4M
  * **Total Interest Earned:** 7.9M
  * **Transaction Amount:** 45M
  * **Transaction Count:** 657K

### Insights Included:

* **Quarterly Revenue & Transaction Trends**

  * Visual comparison of revenue and transaction count across Q1–Q4.

* **Revenue by Card Category**

  * Analysis of revenue generated from:

    * Blue
    * Silver
    * Gold
    * Platinum cards

* **Revenue by Expenditure Type**

  * Tracks customer spending across categories such as:

    * Bills
    * Entertainment
    * Fuel
    * Grocery
    * Food
    * Travel

* **Revenue by Education Level**

  * Identifies revenue contribution based on customer education background.

* **Revenue by Customer Job Type**

  * Compares income generated from customer professions like:

    * Self-employed
    * Businessman
    * Government employees
    * White-collar
    * Blue-collar

* **Revenue by Card Usage Method**

  * Evaluates transaction channels:

    * Swipe
    * Chip
    * Online

* **Customer Acquisition Cost**

  * Measures acquisition cost across different card categories.

### Filters/Slicers:

* Quarter-wise filtering (Q1–Q4)
* Gender selection
* Card category filtering
* Income group segmentation

### Purpose:

This dashboard helps businesses understand:

* Customer spending habits
* Most profitable card categories
* Preferred transaction methods
* Revenue-driving customer segments

---

# 2. Credit Card Customer Report Dashboard

The **Customer Report Dashboard** focuses on customer demographics, income analysis, and behavioral segmentation.

### Key Features:

* Displays important KPIs such as:

  * **Total Revenue:** 55.4M
  * **Total Interest:** 7.9M
  * **Total Income:** 577M
  * **Customer Satisfaction Score (CSS):** 3.19

### Insights Included:

* **Revenue vs Gender**

  * Monthly comparison of revenue generated by male and female customers.

* **Age Group Analysis**

  * Revenue contribution by customer age groups:

    * 20–30
    * 30–40
    * 40–50
    * 50–60
    * 60+

* **Customer Job Analysis**

  * Revenue, transaction amount, and income comparison across occupations.

* **Top 5 States**

  * State-wise revenue contribution analysis.

* **Salary Group Distribution**

  * Revenue comparison based on salary groups:

    * High
    * Mid
    * Low

* **Dependent Count Analysis**

  * Revenue segmentation by number of dependents.

* **Marital Status Analysis**

  * Revenue comparison between:

    * Married
    * Single
    * Unknown

* **Education Level Analysis**

  * Revenue generated across different education backgrounds.

### Filters/Slicers:

* Quarter-wise selection
* Gender filter
* Card category filter
* Income level filter

### Purpose:

This dashboard enables organizations to:

* Understand customer demographics
* Identify high-value customer groups
* Improve marketing strategies
* Enhance customer retention and satisfaction
